What is Paper Quilling?

Paper quilling is a delicate art form that transforms simple strips of paper into intricate, flowing designs. The beauty lies in the way these paper shapes interlace, creating visually stunning patterns that seem to come alive. With its soft curves and vibrant colors, quilling brings a sense of elegance and creativity to even the smallest details.

Here are a few of the skills required to make your paper quilling become absolutely magical:

  1. Fine Motor Skills
    • How well can you really twiddle your thumbs?
    • Watch out for your pinkies, they might get pinched!
  2. Depth Perception
    • Keep your eye on the ball
    • Paper requires a lot of attention, and messing up your depths could make your project look terrible!
